# Rise Ritual

*Pre-desk. Zero open loops. Just observe.*

---

## What This Is

The Rise ritual is what happens between waking up and sitting down to work. It's the boot sequence. The goal is not productivity — it's getting online.

## Steps

1. **Meds** — if applicable
2. **Hydrate** — water before anything else
3. **Move** — walk, stretch, anything that isn't sitting
4. **Eat** — something, anything
5. **Arrive** — sit down, open the system, begin

## Design Principles

- This is NOT a morning routine you have to "complete"
- If you skip steps, that's data, not failure
- The minimum viable version: drink water and sit down
- If this ritual causes dread, redesign it — don't blame yourself
- Merlin can walk you through this if you set up a Rise session preset

## Energy Routing

- 🟢 GREEN: Do all steps. Take your time.
- 🟡 YELLOW: Water + sit down. That's enough.
- 🔴 RED: Just sit down. The system will help from there.
