# Wrap Ritual

*End of day. Wind down. Set up tomorrow.*

---

## What This Is

The Wrap ritual closes the day. It captures what happened, sets tomorrow's direction, and helps you stop working.

## Steps

1. **Run checkpoint** — save all work, capture open loops
2. **Set tomorrow's Thing** — gut feeling, don't overthink it
3. **Prep your space** — coffee pot, clothes out, whatever removes friction from tomorrow morning
4. **Wind down** — melatonin, screen off, whatever your protocol is

## Design Principles

- This should take under 10 minutes
- If you skip it, the system still works — checkpoint is the minimum
- Merlin can walk you through this if you set up a Wrap session preset
- The goal is to STOP, not to plan. Planning happens in the morning.
